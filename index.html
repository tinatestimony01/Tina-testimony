About Tina Testimony

Born on August 12, 1980, in Imo State, Nigeria, Tina Testimony discovered her passion for music and ministry at a very young age. Her journey as a gospel artist is deeply rooted in her unwavering faith and a profound desire to use her talent to glorify God.

Tina's music is more than just entertainment; it's a form of worship and evangelism. Each song is carefully crafted to deliver messages of hope, redemption, and the boundless grace of God. She believes that music is a powerful tool for spiritual transformation and dedicates her life to spreading the Gospel through her unique sound.

Her powerful vocals, heartfelt lyrics, and inspiring stage presence have touched countless lives, making her a beloved figure in the gospel music scene. Tina Testimony continues to be a beacon of light, inspiring believers and drawing new souls to Christ through her anointed ministry.
